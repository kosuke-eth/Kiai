# **🚀 Project Summary: KIAI (The Spirit Engine)**

## **1\. Core Concept**

**"A 100% Legal, Real-Time Sentiment Market Powered by AI x Sui"** Strict gambling laws in Japan make it impossible for ONE Championship to officially adopt global prediction markets. KIAI elegantly leaps over this legal barrier using Sui's technology. It is a next-generation engagement infrastructure that synchronizes fan passion on-chain without any financial risk.

## **2\. User Experience**

**"We don't bet money; we sync passion."** A zero-friction experience for every fan.

* **AI-Driven Micro-Books:** AI agents monitor live matches and dynamically generate ultra-short scenarios in real-time (e.g., *"Will Takeru get a knockdown in the next 60 seconds?"*).
* **Send KIAI (Insight Allocation):** Fans swipe to inject their freely distributed "KIAI" energy into these scenarios, proving their "Combat IQ."
* **Dynamic Rewards:** When fans read the momentum correctly, their Dynamic NFT badge on Sui instantly evolves (e.g., White Belt to Black Belt), unlocking exclusive real-world ONE Championship experiences.

## **3\. Why Sui? (Technical Necessity)**

Combat sports are fast. Only Sui can meet both the extreme speed and Japanese compliance requirements.

* **zkLogin:** Onboards everyday fight fans in 1 second via Google accounts—no wallets, no seed phrases required.
* **Parallel Execution:** When 100,000 fans send KIAI simultaneously during a crucial 60-second window, Sui handles the transaction spike with zero lag, generating real-time crowd consensus.
* **Sponsored Transactions:** The protocol covers all gas fees. Eliminating user financial output makes this a completely free and 100% legal system under Japanese sweepstakes laws.
* **Dynamic Objects:** Allows the AI to instantly create, settle, and destroy countless micro-scenarios as independent on-chain objects.

## **4\. Sustainable Monetization**

Not just a hackathon prototype, but a sustainable business model.

* **B2B Sentiment DaaS (Data as a Service):** Providing ultra-high-resolution on-chain data (*"How exactly did fans react at this specific second?"*) to ONE and its sponsors.
* **B2B Sponsored Scenarios:** Selling naming rights for AI-generated scenarios (e.g., *"The Monster Energy Knockout Book"*).
* **B2C Combat IQ Premium:** A SaaS subscription dashboard offering advanced sentiment analytics for hardcore fans.
